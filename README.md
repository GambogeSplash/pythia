## Overview

On-chain data contains signals that predict market movements — whale wallets accumulating, liquidity shifts, smart money flows. Most traders can't access or interpret this data.

Pythia provides:

- **Predictive Signals** — ML models trained on on-chain patterns
- **Wallet Tracking** — Monitor smart money movements
- **Liquidity Analysis** — See where capital is flowing
- **Alert System** — Get notified on significant signals

---

## Design Thesis

> The chain sees everything. The interface should surface what matters.

Dense information architecture for traders who need signal, not noise.
